Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: April 7, 2025

At The Agent Factory, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request the deletion of your personal data from our systems.

Your Data Deletion Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data that we have collected and stored. This includes:

  • Account information
  • Usage data
  • Content you've created using our application
  • Communication history
  • Any other personal information associated with your use of our application

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data in the following ways:

Option 1: Through Your Account Settings

If you have an account with us, you can request data deletion directly through your account settings:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Navigate to "Settings" > "Privacy & Data"
  3. Select "Delete My Data"
  4. Follow the confirmation steps

Option 2: Submit a Request Form

You can also submit a data deletion request using the form below:

Option 3: Contact Us Directly

You can also email us directly with your data deletion request at:

data-deletion@thagentfactory.ai

Please include your full name and the email address associated with your account in your request.

What Happens After You Submit a Request

After we receive your data deletion request:

  1. We will verify your identity to ensure the request is legitimate
  2. We will process your request within 30 days
  3. We will send you a confirmation email once your data has been deleted
  4. If we need additional information, we will contact you

Data Retention and Exceptions

Please note that we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes. We may also retain cached or archived copies of information about you for a certain period of time.

Third-Party Data

For data that we have shared with third parties (such as Meta), we will forward your deletion request to those third parties where applicable.
